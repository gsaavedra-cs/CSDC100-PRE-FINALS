Using AI as a Co-pilot

Think of AI as a helpful assistant: it can draft code, suggest refactors, and speed up repetitive tasks, but the developer must review and direct its output.

Best practices

  • Verify generated code and tests thoroughly.
  • Keep ownership and responsibility for final outputs.
  • Document where AI assisted significant work.
