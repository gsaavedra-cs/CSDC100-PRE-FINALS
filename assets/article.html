Analogy: Generative AI in Development Work

How developers can adopt Generative AI (GenAI) as a co-pilot to improve productivity while maintaining code quality, ethics, and accountability.

Reflection Scenario

Scenario: You are organizing your wedding, the documentation team is asking 40k for the wedding package. They will use an iPhone 16 pro max to cover the event and CapCut for the same day edit video. The documentation team has 2 years of experience and has an above average portfolio. Would you agree to that deal? Explain your answer and justify the integrity of their work. Reflect on the integrity, creativity, skill level of the “vibe” coders.

Answer: I will accept as long as they deliver the best of the best output without heavy reliance on AI. This means their final deliverables must demonstrate clear human craftsmanship: thoughtful shot selection, careful composition, competent audio capture, and a polished edit that reflects creative choices rather than automated stitching. Ask for references, sample same-day edit clips, and a contract clause specifying quality and revision rounds; prefer teams that show deliberate skill and a workflow where tools like CapCut or AI-assisted edits are used to augment and not replace human judgment and taste.
