Ethical Use of AI

Principles and practical guidelines for responsible AI use.

Responsible use in education

When using AI tools for academic work, disclose assistance, verify outputs, and avoid using them to misrepresent your own work.

Bias, fairness, and verification

Check outputs for bias, verify facts independently, and prefer transparent tools that provide provenance where possible.
