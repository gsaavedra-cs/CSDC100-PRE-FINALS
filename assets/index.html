What is General AI?

General AI (AGI) refers to systems able to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks at a level comparable to humans.

How does it work?

Approaches vary: large-scale learning, modular reasoning systems, memory and continual learning, and hybrids combining symbolic and neural methods are explored.

Benefits

Healthcare, research acceleration, education personalization, and automation of complex tasks are potential benefits when developed responsibly.

Risks

Risks include misuse, bias, economic disruption, and alignment/safety challenges.
