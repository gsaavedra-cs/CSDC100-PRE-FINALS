Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to common questions about GenAI and General AI.

1. What is Generative AI?

Generative AI creates novel content (text, images, audio, video) by learning from large datasets.

2. How does it work?

It uses machine learning models, often deep neural networks, trained to predict and generate outputs based on patterns in data.

3. Examples of tools

Text: ChatGPT; Images: DALL·E, Midjourney; Video: Runway; Audio: AIVA.

4. Strengths & Limitations

Strengths: fast content generation, idea synthesis. Limitations: hallucinations, bias, and occasional inaccuracies.
